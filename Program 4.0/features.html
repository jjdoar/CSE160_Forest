Description:
I implimented a canvas which can spawn red and blue trees when left and right clicked
on. You can then select and deselect individual trees and translate, rotate, and scale them.

I also implimented a point light which is represented by the yellow sphere.
The sphere can be translated using the same controls as the trees.

The final thing I implimented is basic camera controls which allow for panning along all axis
as well as zooming in and out when in perspective mode.

All three of the transformations work, but if you scale too quickly in either direction the other
trees will flicker and sometimes disappear. This can be fixed by slowly scaling in either direction.
This is also more or less likely to happen depending on how many trees are being drawn on screen.

This program was created while running on Google Chrome.


User guide:
Left click on background to place a red tree.
Right click on background to place a blue tree.
Left click on tree to select tree.
Left click on background to deselect a tree.
Left click and drag selected tree to translate along XY plane.
middle mouse click and drag selected tree to translate along Z axis.
Right click and drag horizontally to rotate around the Z axis.
Right click and drag vertically to rotate around the X axis
Sroll wheel up and down to scale.

Left click and drag the selected point light to translate it.
Right click on the selected sphere to turn it on and off.

Left click and drag on the background to pan the camera along the XY plane.
middle click and drag on the background to pan the camera along the Z axis.
Scroll the mouse wheel in perspective mode to zoom in and out.

Save scene by naming a save file and then clicking the save button.
Load scene by selecting a save file and then clicking the load button.
New scene by click clicking the new scene button.
